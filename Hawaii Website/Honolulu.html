Population

Based on the 2020 U.S. Census, the population of Honolulu is about 350,964 people.

Incorporated Year

1907

Region

Honolulu is located in the southern region of the island of Oahu.

Classification

Honolulu is classified as an urban area. It is the largest city in Hawaii and the capital of the state.

Average Income

According to the US Census Bureau, the median household income in Honolulu is approximately $89,800, which is slightly below the state median of around $91,000.

Honolulu is home to Iolani Palace, the only royal palace in the United States!
