Population

Based on the 2020 U.S. Census, Hilo has a population of approximately 44,186 people.

Incorporated Year

Hilo is not an incorporated city.

Region

Hilo is located on the northeastern coast of the Big Island (Hawaiʻi Island).

Classification

Hilo is classified as a mix of urban and rural. The downtown and waterfront areas are urban, while the surrounding neighborhoods and farmland areas give it a rural feel in many parts.

Average Income

The median household income in Hilo is approximately $63,000, which is significantly lower than the state median of about $91,000.

Hilo is one of the rainiest cities in the United States, receiving an average of over 120 inches of rain per year — and in some areas, it can top 200 inches!
