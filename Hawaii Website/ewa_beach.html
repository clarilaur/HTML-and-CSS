Population

Based on the 2020 U.S. Census, Ewa Beach has a population of approximately 16,415 people.

Incorporated Year

Ewa Beach is not an incorporated city.

Region

Ewa Beach is located on the southwestern coast of Oahu, in the Ewa District of Honolulu County, Hawaii.

Classification

Ewa Beach is classified as a suburban community.

Average Income

The median household income in Ewa Beach is approximately $107,000, which is higher than the state median of around $91,000.

Ewa Beach is rich in ancient Hawaiian history and was once known for its extensive fishponds and sugar plantations.
